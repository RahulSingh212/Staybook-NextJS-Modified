Discover the vibrant capital of India - Delhi

1 Day Sightseeing

Enquire

Delhi is diverse in northern India, with a very rich history back to the Mughal era. The city is an epicenter of cultures and religions, making it an interesting place to explore. With historical monuments, religious sites, museums, parks, and markets, Delhi has something special to offer for everyone. we'll take you through some of the top sights to see in Delhi. Delhi, a mix of history and modernity, known for its rich culture, and historical sites, it is the capital city of India and also the capital of so many good things must-see for anyone traveling to India. Historical Attraction and monument: Delhi is the capital of historical monuments and landmarks. Some of the top attractions such as- Red Fort: It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit for anyone interested in Delhi's history that is built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century, the Qutub Minar: It is the world's tallest brick minaret built by Qutubuddin Aiyak in the 12th century, the Qutub Minar is also a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Delhi's most iconic landmarks. India Gate: A war memorial located in the heart of the city, the India Gate is a popular spot for picnics and evening strolls. Humayun's Tomb: Humayun's Tomb is a stunning example of Mughal architecture and one of the city's most visited sites. You might not even know that Humayun Tomb is the real inspiration behind Taj Mahal. Jama Masjid: India's largest mosque, the Jama Masjid is fascinating Islamic architecture also built by Mughal emperor Shahjahan in 1650 to 1656 and a must-visit for anyone interested in Delhi's religious history. Religious Places: Akshardham Temple: The temple spread over 100 acres with beautiful pink traditional architecture, dedicated to the swaminarayan built in the 19 century to depict the methodology of hinduism. Prayer also can enjoy temples surrounded by beautiful gardens, light shows, attending multiple exhibitions, and enjoying boat rides. Lotus Temple: The beautiful lotus shaped temple with 27 white marble petals built by the Baha'i community in 1986 is open for all religions that shows the unity of all religions and equality of all people. The Interior of this majestic temple is very large that can accommodate 2500 people at a time.There are no idols or religious symbols, Prayer can meditate or pray in silence. Gurudwara Bangla Sahib: The prominent sikh gurdwara spread over a large area, its stunning architecture, which is a mix of mughal and sikh art. It also offers a langar that serves free meals to thousands of devotees despite any discrimination of caste and religion, The temple is also a very popular destination for tourists and devotees. Iskcon temple: Also known as lord krishna temple built in 1988, Iskcon is a franchise of temples that is located in several cities such as Delhi, Mumbai, Noida, and Hyderabad. Kalkaji Temple: Also known as Kalkaji Mandir, dedicated to Goddess Kali, located in South Delhi, built in the 18th century. The temple is one of the oldest and most visited temples in Delhi. Hazrat Nizamuddin Dargah: It is a very popular Dargah among muslims, It is named after the famous Sufi saint, Hazrat Nizamuddin Auliya, who lived in the area during the 14th century. The dargah is one of the most important pilgrimage sites for Muslims and it attracts thousands of devotees, Consists of several tombs, including the tomb of Hazrat Nizamuddin Auliya, and Hazrat Amir Khushro. Shopping and Market: Delhi is a shopping destination among travelers, with a several variety of markets and shopping destinations at all budgets. Here are some most popular shopping market in Delhi: Chandni Chowk: It is one of the oldest and busiest markets in Delhi, with a slightly narrow street that is lined with shops from clothes and jewelry to spices and street food. Also known for traditional Indian wear and is a must-visit for anyone looking for a unique shopping experience. Connaught Place: popularly known CP, it is one of Delhi's most famous shopping marketplace. It is a circular market that is divided into blocks. From high-end malls to street vendors, Connaught Place has something for everyone. Sarojini Nagar Market: Sarojini Nagar Market is a popular destination for affordable shopping. It is famous for its clothes, shoes, jewelry, electronics, and accessories, available at bargain prices. The market is especially popular with young adults and students. Lajpat Nagar Market: A mix of traditional and modern shops, selling everything from Indian wear and accessories to electronics and home décor, Lajpat Nagar Market is another popular destination for shopping in Delhi, especially popular during the festive season when it is decorated with lights. Dilli Haat: Dilli Haat is a unique open-air market that showcases the best of Indian handicrafts and cuisine. Food and Restaurants: Each city has a different taste and unique speciality. Delhi is also the destination of many foods such as chole bhature, banke bihari ke samose and rashgulle, Chawla de chur-chur naan, Kabab, Butter Chicken, Momos, and many more with a variety of cuisines and restaurants that cater to all tastes and budgets. Here are some of the best places to eat in Delhi: Karim: Karim's is one of Delhi's most famous restaurants and is known for its delicious Mughlai cuisine. Located in Old Delhi, Karim's has been serving laziz kebabs, biryanis, and curries for over a century. Parathe Wali Gali: Parathe Wali Gali is a narrow street in Old Delhi that is lined with shops selling a variety of stuffed parathas. From spicy potato and onion parathas to sweet parathas this street is a must-visit for anyone looking to sample some authentic Delhi street food. Rajendra De Dhaba: Nightlife and entertainment : Hauz Khas Village: Hauz Khas Village is a popular destination for nightlife in Delhi. The area is filled with trendy bars, beautiful lakes, parks, cafes, and restaurants that serve to all tastes and budgets. Connaught Place: Connaught Place is not only a shopping destination but the area is filled with bars, clubs, and pubs that serve all budgets. From high-end nightclubs to budget-friendly bars, Connaught Place always has something for everyone. Hard Rock Cafe: Popular chain of restaurants and bars that is known for its live music performances. The Delhi location is no different, with regular performances by local and international artists. PVR Cinema: Popular chain of multiplex cinemas that is known for its luxurious seating and high-quality unforgettable experience. The chain has multiple locations across Delhi such as connaught place, Rivoli, Chanakyapuri, Nraina, Shalimarbagh, Shubhas nagar and is a great place to catch the latest Bollywood and Hollywood films.
